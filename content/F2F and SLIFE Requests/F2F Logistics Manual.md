For you to see what exactly you need to file, you may consult the master sheet, specifically this sheet [Face to Face Logistics Responses Page](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1P1Hvs30_ibmVP5AOZX-O9RvQGOwc5kig4ASa_7dsna0/edit?gid=359764443#gid=359764443)

After that, you need to login into [The Concierge website.](https://theconcierge.dlsu.edu.ph/support/home)

Login with your DLSU Account. 

You can use the C[oncierge Processing Guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1KNpR98WzFp9gXIAM74kFKs1VWnkV04W2TxlM12wqduo/edit?usp=sharing) for any of the following: 
1. FSO (formerly BGMO)
2. FEP (Food Entry Permit)
3. AVSU (Audio-Visual Unit; but not including projectors)
4. MME (Multi-Media Equipment; including projectors and laptops)
